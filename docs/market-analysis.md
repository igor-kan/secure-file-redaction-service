# Market Analysis

## Project
Secure File Redaction Service

## Market Segment
Compliance-oriented redaction and privacy-preserving document handling.

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

- Mid-size to enterprise organizations with compliance, auditability, and workflow automation needs.
- Public sector, legal/regulatory, operations, and back-office digitization teams.

## Buyer Pains

- Manual, inconsistent workflows with high error rates.
- Slow processing and lack of SLA visibility.
- Limited auditability and weak governance controls.
- Integration friction across identity, document, and workflow stacks.

## Monetization Options

- SaaS subscription by seats + usage.
- Enterprise tier with SSO, audit exports, private networking, and support SLAs.
- API/automation usage pricing for high-volume workloads.

## Go-To-Market Options

- Portfolio-led selling via technical case studies and demo environments.
- Partner-led adoption through systems integrators.
- Pilot deployments with measured time-savings and quality uplift KPIs.

## Near-Term Market Priorities

- Convert demos into production-like reference implementations.
- Expand compliance narrative (least privilege, audit trace, data handling).
- Publish benchmark metrics (latency, throughput, review reduction).
